Popular Destinations

Chandigarh-Beautiful City

Explore Chandigarh, the beautiful Tricity known for its stunning architecture, lush gardens, and serene lakes. A perfect blend of nature and modernity, it’s a must-visit destination for travelers!

Shimla-The Queen of Hills

Shimla, with its snowy peaks, colonial charm, and scenic landscapes, is perfect for nature lovers and adventure seekers. Explore Mall Road, Kufri for an unforgettable getaway!

Delhi-The Heart of India

Discover Delhi, the Heart of India, where history and modernity blend seamlessly. From majestic monuments to bustling bazaars, this vibrant city has something for every traveler!

Goa-Pearl of the Orient

Goa, the ultimate beach paradise, offers golden sands, vibrant nightlife, and rich Portuguese heritage. Whether you seek adventure or relaxation, this coastal gem has it all!

Savor & Stroll

The Aroma

A timeless landmark in the City Beautiful, Hotel Aroma blends classic charm with modern comforts, offering a perfect stay in the heart of Chandigarh!

Eighteen71

Step into the colonial charm of Eighteen71, where timeless elegance meets exquisite flavors, all against the stunning backdrop of Shimla’s hills.

Goofa ashiana

Iconic open-air dining with delicious flavors and breathtaking panoramas—every bite is a scenic delight!

Cafe Simla Times

Awarded Himachal’s Most Instagrammable Café, this scenic gem blends artisanal flavors with picture-perfect charm, making every sip a memory.

Featured Activities

Tide & Glide

Dive into Goa’s thrill—jet skiing, parasailing, and scuba diving turn every wave into an adventure. Feel the rush, embrace the splash!

Lights & vibes

Delhi’s nightlife dazzles with electrifying clubs, rooftop lounges, and street food delights—where every night is a celebration!

Whispering Winds

Feel the adrenaline rush at Thunder Zone with wild rides, then soar and flip at Chandigarh’s Trampoline Park for endless fun!

Frozen Paradise

Shimla’s snowy slopes offer thrilling skiing, ice skating, snowboarding, winter camping and paragliding over stunning snow-kissed valleys!
